# AI-Powered-Quality-Control-for-Attar-Glass-Bottles
Building AI course project
## Summary
This project uses AI to automatically detect defects in small glass attar bottles. It helps identify cracks, bubbles, or shape issues during production to ensure high quality.
## Background
• In glass manufacturing, manual checking is slow and misses small defects.
• High furnace temperatures (using soda ash and borax) can sometimes cause inconsistent batches.
• My goal is to reduce waste and ensure every attar bottle is perfect for the customer.
## How is it used?
A camera is installed on the conveyor belt. The AI scans each bottle as it passes. If a defect is found, the system alerts the worker or marks the bottle for removal.
## Data sources and AI methods
• Data: Images of good bottles and bottles with common defects.
• AI Method: Computer Vision (Convolutional Neural Networks) for image classification.
## Challenges
• High heat and dust in the factory can affect camera performance.
• The system must process images very fast to keep up with production.
## What next?
The AI could eventually connect to the furnace controls to suggest temperature adjustments based on the defect rate.
